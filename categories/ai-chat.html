AI Chat Tools

AI chat tools are the fastest entry point into practical AI use. They help with drafting, rewriting, summarising, analysis, coding support, and everyday Q&A. The category is crowded, so the real job is not finding tools — it is narrowing down the right one for your workflow.

Open full directory

What this category is best for

If you need a general assistant for mixed work, start with ChatGPT or Claude. If your workflow is research-heavy, source-backed, or web-aware, look at Perplexity. If you already live inside Google Workspace, Gemini is often the easiest fit.

Use compare when you care about long-context quality, citation quality, mobile availability, API access, export options, or enterprise readiness.

How to choose quickly

  1. Writing + broad tasks: ChatGPT
  2. Long documents + careful analysis: Claude
  3. Research + citations: Perplexity
  4. Workspace users: Gemini
  5. Reflective / lightweight conversation: Pi
