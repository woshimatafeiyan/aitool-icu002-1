What to compare before you pick a tool

Output quality

Can it actually produce work you can use, or does it only look impressive in demos?

Workflow fit

Does it fit how you already work, or will it add more switching and overhead?

Pricing

Free and freemium are useful, but only if the important parts are not locked away.

Reliability

For work use, stable output and clear limits usually matter more than flashy features.
